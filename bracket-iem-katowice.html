How the Draw Worked
Two GSL groups feed eight teams into the playoff bracket

All eight playoff slots were earned in the Studio EXPO group stage that ran Tuesday through Thursday. The two group winners (A1 Vitality, B1 Spirit) were placed into opposite halves of the draw and matched against the cross-group fourth seeds. Group runners-up faced third seeds from the other group, with the upper bracket loser of every group rerouted into the lower bracket round.

Vitality opened on the main stage at noon Friday against MOUZ, the second seed out of Group B. Spirit and Liquid followed in the second QF slot. Saturday's afternoon session paired NaVi with Astralis and FaZe with Heroic. Semifinals rolled into Saturday night and Sunday morning, with the Bo5 grand final closing out the Spodek crowd at 18:00 CET on Sunday.
