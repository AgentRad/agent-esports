Dignitas is an American esports organization founded September 8, 2003 and headquartered in New Jersey. The org is one of the longest-running in esports history, with active divisions across multiple major titles. Its Fortnite division is currently the #2 organization in global Power Index (88.2 PI).

The Dignitas Fortnite duo of Khanada and VicterV won the FNCS 2026 Major 1 NA West Grand Finals by a 160-point margin in April 2026, taking home $30,000 each. They are competing at the Major 1 Summit alongside teammate Rapid as part of the Dignitas trio (currently 3rd at 710 points).
