Organization Overview
Saudi-backed multi-game powerhouse

Team Falcons is a Saudi Arabian esports organization headquartered in Riyadh. Founded in 2017, the org has expanded aggressively since 2023 with backing from Public Investment Fund-affiliated capital, becoming the most active esports organization in the world by roster count and acquisition pace.

In Fortnite specifically, Falcons fields rosters in North America and Europe. They signed Peterbot in December 2024 and Pollo in February 2026, reuniting the duo that won the 2024 FNCS Global Championship. The trio of Pollo, Acorn, and Ajerss currently leads the FNCS 2026 Major 1 Summit.

Recent moves: TaySon departed January 20, 2026; T3eny, Chico, and Japko joined the EU squad May 28, 2026 (Japko later parted ways March 2026). Vanyak (17, France) is one of their youngest signings, anchoring a long-term EU project.
