m0NESY

From AgentTV, the serious source for esports. Last updated May 7, 2026 by AGENT staff.

Ilya Osipov (Илья Осипов; born May 1, 2005), known online as m0NESY, is a Russian professional Counter-Strike 2 player currently signed to Team Falcons. A primary AWPer regarded as the defining sniper of the post-s1mple generation, he was named HLTV's second-best player of 2024 at age 19, the youngest top-two finish in the rankings' history.[1][2]

Osipov rose through the Natus Vincere youth system before being acquired by G2 Esports in early 2022, where he spent more than three years as the centerpiece of the European roster. He moved to Team Falcons on April 14, 2025 in one of the largest CS2 transfer deals on record. He has accumulated approximately $957,000 in tracked earnings and holds 6 tournament MVPs.[1][3]

Early life

Osipov was born May 1, 2005 in Moscow, Russia. He began playing Counter-Strike: Global Offensive at age 8 and was widely regarded as a Russian-language matchmaking phenom by his early teens, drawing comparisons to a young s1mple. He signed his first professional contract at 14.[1]

Career

NaVi Junior (2020 to 2021)

Osipov played briefly for NewBALLS and S-Gaming before joining the Natus Vincere youth pipeline in January 2020 and being promoted to NaVi Junior on March 11, 2021. He left the academy roster on December 8, 2021 to pursue a tier-one move ahead of his sixteenth birthday.[1]

G2 Esports (2022 to 2025)

On January 3, 2022, Osipov signed with G2 Esports to take over the AWP role from Aleksib-era teammates. Within his rookie year he won BLAST Premier World Final 2022 and finished HLTV #7. He climbed to #4 in 2023, peaked at #2 in 2024 with five MVPs in a single calendar year (IEM Dallas, BLAST Fall Final, BLAST World Final, and others), and finished #4 in 2025. G2 reached the grand final of multiple S-Tier events and won IEM Cologne 2023 alongside NiKo.[2][3]

Team Falcons (2025 to present)

On April 14, 2025, m0NESY transferred to Team Falcons, joining a stacked Saudi-backed roster reportedly for a multi-million-dollar buyout. The Falcons project has been built around his AWPing alongside other former top-five HLTV players. The team has been a regular S-Tier semifinalist in the post-Austin Major calendar.[4]

Legacy and awards

m0NESY's six tournament MVP medals (BLAST World Final 2022, IEM Dallas 2024, BLAST Fall Final 2024, BLAST World Final 2024, ESL Pro League Season 22, BLAST Rivals Fall 2025) include four Big-Event MVPs. He is widely viewed as the natural heir to s1mple and ZywOo in the AWP lineage and consistently posts the highest opening-pick win rate among elite snipers since 2023.[2][3]

He maintains an active streaming presence on Twitch under the handle m0nesyof and is one of the most-followed Russian-speaking CS2 streamers, with significant cross-pollination from VKontakte and Telegram audiences.

Personal life

Osipov resides in Moscow but has bootcamped extensively in Belgrade and Berlin during his G2 and Falcons tenures. He has been transparent in interviews about the unusual pressure of being labeled a generational AWPer from age 15, and has credited his time in the NaVi Junior environment with much of his structured tactical development.[1]
