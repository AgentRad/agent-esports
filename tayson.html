TaySon

From AgentTV, the serious source for esports. Last updated May 7, 2026 by AGENT staff.

Tai Starčič (born June 9, 2004), known online as TaySon, is a Slovenian professional Fortnite Battle Royale player. One of the longest-tenured EU players still active, he competed for Team Falcons from July 1, 2021 through January 20, 2026 · the longest single-org tenure of any current EU competitor. With approximately $1,338,052 in lifetime tournament earnings, he is among the highest-earning Slovenian esports athletes of any title.[1]

TaySon's resume includes five regional FNCS Grand Finals titles and a 1st-place finish at DreamHack Dallas 2024. He departed Falcons in January 2026 amid a roster restructuring and is currently listed as inactive as of April 2026.

Competitive career

TaySon broke through during the Chapter 2 era, winning the C2S3 EU FNCS Grand Finals (2020, $80,000) and C2S4 EU FNCS Grand Finals ($37,000). He went on to win the 2021 FNCS Grand Royale ($200,000) and the FNCS 2023 Major 1: Europe ($100,000), establishing himself as one of the most consistent EU competitors of the 2021-2024 stretch.[1]

His Falcons era (July 2021 - January 2026) ran 4.5 years, during which he picked up an additional DreamHack Dallas 2024 title ($25,000). He departed Falcons on January 20, 2026 and has been inactive since April 2026.
