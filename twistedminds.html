Twisted Minds is a Saudi Arabian esports organization founded in 2021 and headquartered in Riyadh. The organization established its Fortnite division on November 23, 2022 and has since accumulated approximately $1,388,249 in tracked Fortnite earnings · placing it among the top-funded teams in Middle Eastern esports.

On February 7, 2026 the organization announced the signing of Canadian competitor Acorn, expanding its NA presence ahead of the 2026 FNCS season. Acorn joins existing US players Cold and Boltz alongside Canadian Rapid (signed March 1, 2026).
