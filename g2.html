Organization Overview
The most successful Western League of Legends organization

G2 Esports is a German esports organization headquartered in Berlin. Founded in February 2014 by Spanish former pro player Carlos "ocelote" Rodriguez, the team purchased an LEC franchise slot in the league's transition to a permanent partnership model in 2018 and has remained the most decorated Western LoL organization since.

G2 has won the LEC and EU LCS a total of 12 times, more than any other organization in league history. The team's most successful season was 2019, when the super-team of Wunder, Jankos, Perkz, Caps and Mikyx won the 2019 Mid-Season Invitational in Vietnam (the first and only Western team to win MSI) and reached the Worlds 2019 final in Paris, where they lost 0-3 to FunPlus Phoenix.

The organization fields rosters across League of Legends, Valorant, CS2, Rocket League, Sim Racing and Fortnite. Caps remains the team's franchise mid laner, having signed a three-year contract extension in April 2026.
