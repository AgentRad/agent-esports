Clix

From AgentTV, the serious source for esports.

Cody Conrod (born January 7, 2005), known online as Clix, is an American-Italian professional Fortnite player and Twitch streamer currently competing for XSET. He holds dual U.S. and Italian citizenship and has accumulated approximately $798,832 in tournament earnings since 2019.[1]

Clix won the 2024 Streamer of the Year award at The Streamer Awards (Fortnite category), beating Peterbot among others. He is one of the few players to consistently rank among both the top streamers and the top competitors in the game.

Early life

Conrod was born January 7, 2005 with dual American and Italian citizenship. He picked up Fortnite during the early Chapter 1 era and qualified for his first competitive event at age 13.

NRG era and breakthrough

Conrod competed for NRG from 2019 through 2022, becoming one of the org's most prominent figures during the Chapter 2 trios era. Multiple deep FNCS finishes during this period built his reputation as one of the best young NA East players.

XSET era

After leaving NRG, Conrod signed with XSET in 2023 and remains with the organization as of 2026. He has competed in the FNCS 2025 Global Championship and continues to make deep regional Major runs.

Streaming and content

Clix's Twitch channel reached 5.8 million followers by 2024, and he has been at the center of multiple high-profile streamer feuds and crossovers. His fanbase, dubbed the "Clix Nation," is among the most engaged in Fortnite content.
