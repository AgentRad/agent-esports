TSM (originally Team SoloMid) is an American esports organization founded in September 2009. With approximately $2,202,165 in tracked Fortnite earnings across its 2018-2024 division and a reactivated 2026 roster, TSM remains one of the longest-running and best-known organizations in North American esports. Headquartered in the United States and active across multiple titles · Apex Legends, VALORANT, CS2, Smash, and Fortnite · the organization initially disbanded its Fortnite division on September 19, 2024 before reactivating in early 2026 around the Lucid and Cold duo.

TSM's Fortnite history dates back to January 2018 when the org signed Myth · one of the most-followed early Fortnite streamers · followed by ZexRow, Commandment, and later EpikWhale, who joined in 2022 before transferring to FaZe Clan in 2023.
