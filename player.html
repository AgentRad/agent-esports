Career Bio
Sourced from official records and verified interviews

Peter "Peterbot" Kata is a Hungarian-American Fortnite professional currently competing for Team Falcons. Born June 20, 2007 in the United States to Hungarian parents, Kata began competing in 2020 and qualified for his first FNCS Grand Finals in Chapter 2 Season 7 (2021).

His breakout came in 2024, when he and partner Moreno won the 2024 FNCS Global Championship for $200,000 each · cementing his place among the best players of his generation. He has accumulated over $1.21M in lifetime tournament earnings across 110+ events.

In 2026, after a brief split, Peterbot reunited with Pollo for the FNCS duo format and is the consensus favorite to win Major 1 Summit. He streams to over 951,000 followers on Twitch and was nominated for the 2024 Streamer of the Year at the Streamer Awards.
