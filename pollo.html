Pollo

From AGENT, the serious source for esports. Last updated May 6, 2026.

Miguel Moreno (born May 8, 2008), known online as Pollo, is an American-Mexican professional Fortnite Battle Royale player currently competing for Team Falcons. Having won four FNCS titles · including the 2024 FNCS Global Championship alongside duo partner Peterbot · he is widely regarded as one of the best Fortnite players of recent years.[1]

At age 17, Pollo currently sits at #2 in global Power Rankings (3,201 PR), trailing only his duo partner Peterbot. The pair are leading the FNCS 2026 Major 1 Summit as of May 6, 2026. He maintains an active streaming presence on Twitch and X (Twitter) as @pollofn6.

Early life

Moreno was born May 8, 2008 to Mexican parents living in the United States. He picked up Fortnite during the Chapter 2 era and began competing in NA East cash cups by age 13.[2]

Competitive career

Early career (2022-2023)

Moreno first qualified for an FNCS regional final in 2022, competing in the duos format introduced with Chapter 3 Season 1. He partnered briefly with several NA East players before joining FaZe Clan in late 2023, around the same time as a young player named Peter "Peterbot" Kata.

FaZe Clan era (2023-2024)

The Pollo + Peterbot duo became one of the most dominant partnerships in modern Fortnite. The pair won the 2024 FNCS Major 3 NA East in July 2024 ($60,000 to 1st duo), and capped the year by winning the 2024 FNCS Global Championship in Copenhagen · taking home $200,000 each.[3]

Solo year (2025)

Pollo and Peterbot temporarily split during the 2025 season, with Moreno briefly competing for European org Gentle Mates as a solo+duo partner with various players. He placed 2nd at the 2025 Global Championship in Antwerp under Gentle Mates colors before reuniting with Peterbot for the 2026 season.

Falcons era (2026-)

Moreno joined Team Falcons on February 6, 2026, reuniting with Peterbot who had signed with the Saudi-backed organization in December 2024. The duo's reunion was widely covered as the most consequential roster move of the 2026 offseason.[4]

Currently, Pollo is partnered with Acorn (also of Team Falcons) for the FNCS 2026 Major 1 Summit, where the duo leads the standings with 770 points heading into Game 12.

Notable partnerships

Pollo's career has been defined by partnerships with elite NA East talent:

  • Peterbot (FaZe 2023-2024, Falcons 2026-) · 2024 FNCS Global Champion
  • Acorn (Falcons 2026 Major 1 Summit) · current 770 pt leaders
  • Various Gentle Mates players (2025) · 2nd place Global Championship
