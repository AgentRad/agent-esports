Flickzy

From AgentTV, the serious source for esports.

Felix G. (born May 9, 2006), known online as Flickzy, is a German professional Fortnite player currently competing for GodLike Esports (signed April 25, 2026). With approximately $559,070 in lifetime tournament earnings, he is among the top EU competitors of the 2024 to 2026 era.[1]

Flickzy is a two-time FNCS Major EU regional champion: 2024 Major 3 EU ($85,000) and 2025 Major 1 EU ($60,000). He competed for AIGHT from June 2022 through January 2026 before joining GodLike Esports.
