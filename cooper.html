Cooper

From AgentTV, the serious source for esports. Last updated May 7, 2026 by AGENT staff.

Cooper Cole Smith (born January 7, 2007), known online as Cooper, is an American professional Fortnite Battle Royale player currently competing for Dignitas. He won the 2023 FNCS Global Championship at age 16 alongside Canadian IGL Mero, taking home $500,000 each (over $1 million total prize for the duo).[1]

Cooper signed with Dignitas on February 16, 2024 and remains on the org's NA roster as of 2026. With approximately $784,290 in lifetime tournament earnings, he is one of the top young earners in NA Fortnite.

Competitive career

Cooper rose through the FNCS qualifier system in 2022 before being paired with Mero for the 2023 competitive cycle. The duo's victory at the FNCS 2023 Global Championship · with Cooper still 16 years old · made him one of the youngest GC winners in Fortnite history.[1]

After Mero's transition to a cross-region duo with Andilex in 2025, Cooper rotated to a new partnership and continued competing for Dignitas alongside teammates like Khanada.
