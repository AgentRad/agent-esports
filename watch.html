How AgentTV Watch Works
Every broadcast on AgentTV is loaded through the official Twitch player. We pass the channel slug into the player URL and the streamer keeps full control of the experience: ads play, subscriber chat works, monetization is unaffected. We do not capture or rebroadcast video, and we do not store stream content. If you'd rather watch on the source platform, hit the "Open on Twitch" button next to the player.
