NRG (NRG Esports) is an American esports organization founded April 6, 2015 by Andy Miller, former owner of the Sacramento Kings, alongside Mark Mastrov. Headquartered in Los Angeles with significant Bay Area presence, NRG fields competitive teams across multiple major titles.

NRG's Fortnite division is one of the most decorated in the game's history. EpikWhale placed 3rd at the 2019 World Cup Solo Final under the org banner ($1.2M). The org also signed Benjyfishy (now Valorant) and Clix in their early years.

Active Roster

Mixed NA-East and NA-West roster with 6 players. Notable current players include Curve and Veno, who won the FNCS 2026 Major 1 NA Central Grand Finals with 727 points.

Notable Alumni

  • EpikWhale · 2018 to 2024 · 2019 WC Solo 3rd
  • Benjyfishy · former Fortnite division · now Valorant Heretics
