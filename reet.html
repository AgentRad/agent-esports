Reet

From AgentTV, the serious source for esports. Last updated May 7, 2026 by AGENT staff.

Nathan Allen Amundson (born May 16, 2006), known online as Reet, is an American professional Fortnite Battle Royale player from the NA East region. He is best known as the longtime trios mate of Bugha and Mero on Sentinels, and currently competes for JOGO. With approximately $508,927 in lifetime tournament earnings, he is among the most consistent NA trios competitors.[1]

Reet's signature event was the C2S8 FNCS Grand Finals (NA-W qualifier) in 2021, which his trio won. He has also collected multiple 2nd-place finishes at FNCS Majors across 2022 and 2024.

Competitive career

Reet entered the competitive scene as a teenager during the Chapter 2 era. He paired with Bugha and Mero on Sentinels, forming what became one of the most recognizable NA East trios from 2021-2023. The trio's biggest result was 1st at C2S8 FNCS Grand Finals in 2021 ($45,000 each), followed by 1st at the FNCS 2021 Grand Royale.[1]

After the FNCS pivoted to duos-only formats in 2023, Reet rotated through several short-term duo partners before signing with JOGO in mid-2025. He famously paired with Bugha in a Quinn x Kylie $5,000 Trios event in 2021.
