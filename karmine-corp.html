Organization Overview
French esports brand · Vatira's competitive home since 2022

Karmine Corp is a French esports organisation co-founded by Kameto (Kamel Kebir) and Kotei (Zouhair Darji) in March 2020 and registered as a competitive entity in subsequent years. The Rocket League division was launched on July 26, 2021 with a roster of European veterans aimed at the post-Psyonix Epic-published era.

The roster underwent a structural rebuild in late 2022 when the organisation signed teenage striker Vatira alongside Atow. and juicy, the trio that has since stayed largely intact under head coach Extra (Alexandre Paoli). The combination became one of the longest-running tier-one rosters of the post-Psyonix era and the structural anchor of the European competitive scene.

Karmine has lifted two RLCS Majors (the RLCS 2022-23 Winter Major and the RLCS 2025 Birmingham Major), reached the top four at every World Championship from 2022 onwards, and accumulated approximately $1.86M in tracked Rocket League earnings, placing them in the top-three active organisations by lifetime RL prize money.
