Organization Overview
Legendary North American esports brand

Evil Geniuses (EG) is one of the oldest active esports organizations in the world, founded in Canada in 1999 and now headquartered in the United States. The org joined Valorant on January 27, 2021 and was a founding partner of VCT Americas League in 2023.

EG's Valorant chapter is defined by one of the deepest underdog runs in esports history. Their inaugural Americas League roster of Demon1, Boostio, jawgemo, C0M and Ethan finished bottom of the regular season standings, then ran through the bracket to win VALORANT Champions 2023 in Los Angeles, defeating Paper Rex 3-1 for the $1,000,000 winner share.

The 2026 roster is rebuilt around supamen, dgzin and bao, with assistant coaches pho and Faded continuing from the 2025 cycle.
