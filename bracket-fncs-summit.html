Qualifier Path · 2026 Season

FNCS 2026 Major 1
Qualifier Path

Fortnite scores teams on points, not brackets, so the road to the Major 1 Summit is a funnel of weekly Cash Cups feeding eight Region Finals, which feed the Global Summit, which feeds the year-end Global Championship.
Fortnite Hub All Tournaments
Major 1 Funnel · Open Qualifiers → Region Finals → Summit
Stage 1 · Weekly
Open Cash Cups
All players · All eight regions · 6 weeks
Three weekly Cash Cups (Solos, Duos, Trios) run through every region during the lead-in to a Major. Top duos collect leaderboard points across all six weeks. The cumulative leaderboard at the end of week 6 sets the seeding for the Region Finals invite list. No qualifiers, no entry fee, anyone hits Champion League and shows up.

Stage 2 · Three Weekend Sessions
Region Finals
Six matches per session · 18 matches total
Each region runs three Saturday sessions across the Major window. Six matches per Saturday, 18 matches total, with consistency rewarded over a single hot lobby. Points carry forward across all three weeks. The top duos on the cumulative leaderboard each region split a regional prize pool and lock the top slots for the Major 1 Summit. Final standings settle the regional Major 1 trophy and Open Series finals seeding.

Stage 3 · LAN Summit
Major 1 Global Summit
40 duos · 8 matches · Two days · Berlin
The Major 1 Summit is a two-day, eight-match LAN run. All 40 qualified duos play together in shared servers, so a Brazilian duo can land at Tilted next to a Japanese duo for the first time on stage. Points are awarded for placement and eliminations across all eight matches. The cumulative leaderboard winner takes the Major 1 title and the largest single prize-pool slice on the Fortnite calendar to date.
  • Day 1: Matches 1-4 · Mix-region group lobbies, no respawn rules
  • Day 2: Matches 5-8 · Combined leaderboard sets seeding into the final lobby
  • Trophy: Major 1 plate plus 200,000 FNCS Series Points toward Global Championship qualification
Endgame · Year-End
FNCS Global Championship
Top 50 duos by season points · LAN finale
Series Points earned in Major 1, Major 2 and Major 3 stack into the Global Championship leaderboard. The top 50 duos by season point totals are flown to the year-end finale. There is no last-chance qualifier. Major performance is the only path. Major 1 effectively decides the first quarter of who shows up in December.
Why a Funnel and Not a Bracket
Battle royale points, not single elimination

Fortnite is a 100-player battle royale, so traditional brackets do not apply. Epic Games scores duos across multiple matches in a session, awarding placement points for surviving deep into a lobby and elimination points for kills. Consistency is rewarded over single hot games. The 2026 calendar follows the same rhythm Epic introduced in 2024, with three Majors capped by a year-end Global Championship.

Major 1 is the first chance for a duo to put points on the board, so the open qualifiers leading in are watched closely. Cash Cup leaderboards do not reset between weeks, which means a clean week 1 carries a duo straight to a top seed in the Region Finals invite list and then the Summit.
