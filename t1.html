Organization Overview
The most decorated organization in League of Legends history

T1 is a South Korean esports organization headquartered in Seoul. Originally founded in 2002 as SK Telecom T1 as a StarCraft team, the org entered League of Legends in 2012 and has remained the dominant force of the LCK ever since. T1 was rebranded from SK Telecom T1 in February 2019 following a joint-venture investment between SK Telecom and Comcast Spectacor.

T1 is the only organization to win the League of Legends World Championship six times, with titles in 2013, 2015, 2016, 2023, 2024 and 2025. The 2023 to 2025 run constitutes the first World Championship three-peat since SKT's near-three-peat in 2015 to 2016. The team is built around Faker, who is also a part-owner of the organization.

T1 fields rosters in League of Legends, Valorant, Dota 2, PUBG Mobile, Wild Rift, StarCraft II and Hearthstone. The League of Legends division remains the org's flagship and most decorated team.
