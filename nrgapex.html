Organization Overview
One of the longest-tenured NA orgs in Apex

NRG Esports entered Apex Legends on February 11, 2019, less than two weeks after the game's launch, and remained a fixture of the NA scene for more than six years.

The defining era for the org was the 2021-2023 stretch built around Sweetdreams, Gild and Nafen, a trio that finished 2nd at the 2023 ALGS Year 3 Split 1 Playoffs ($160,000) and qualified for every ALGS LAN of the period. NRG also won the 2021 ALGS Winter Circuit Playoffs NA ($48,000).

NRG parted ways with the Apex roster on August 9, 2025 and the organization stepped back from competitive Apex for the remainder of the season. As of May 2026 the org has not announced a return.
