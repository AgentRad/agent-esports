Sentinels is an American esports organization founded in 2018 by Rob Moore and Stephen Cheek. Headquartered in Los Angeles, the org is best known for its dominant VALORANT division (with TenZ as the centerpiece), but its Fortnite division produced the most iconic moment in competitive Fortnite history: Bugha's 2019 World Cup Solo championship.

Bugha competed for Sentinels from 2018 to 2024, a six-year tenure that made him synonymous with the org. After Bugha's departure to Dignitas (briefly) and his subsequent retirement to streaming, Sentinels' Fortnite roster reduced in size but remains active in NA East competition.
