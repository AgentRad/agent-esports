Organization Overview
Greenwall · the most decorated brand in CoD history

OpTic Texas is the Dallas-based franchise of the Call of Duty League, owned by Hector "H3CZ" Rodriguez's OpTic Gaming organisation. The franchise was unveiled on November 8, 2021 as the rebrand of OpTic Chicago, completing a structural realignment that returned the Greenwall brand to the league after the 2020 franchise cycle.

OpTic Texas became the first organisation in Call of Duty League history to win back-to-back Championships, lifting the trophy at CDL Championship 2024 in Allen, Texas and CDL Championship 2025. The 2024 ring came on the back of a 5-1 grand final win over New York Subliners, with Shotzzy named Finals MVP. The franchise also captured the Esports World Cup 2025 Call of Duty bracket in Riyadh.

The current 4-man core of Shotzzy, Dashy, Huke and Mercules, coached by Karma, is widely regarded as the cleanest structural fit of the franchise era. The team enters the 2026 season as the favourite to threepeat at CDL Champs.
