About the Tournament
Burbank Studios · June 16 to 19, 2024

CDL Major IV 2024 was the fourth and final regular-season Major of the Call of Duty League's Modern Warfare III calendar. The Burbank stop was hosted by the Los Angeles Thieves franchise inside the Burbank Studios soundstage, with the bracket drawn directly from the league's regular-season points race and seeded eight teams onto the LAN floor.

The headline storyline coming in was Atlanta FaZe's bid to lock the season's fourth Major final from a possible four. Atlanta had finished runner-up to Toronto Ultra at Major I, won Major II over OpTic Texas 4-1 and watched OpTic Texas win Major III on FaZe's home soil. The team arrived in Burbank as the league's top regular-season points earner.

The New York Subliners had a quietly strong run-up. The roster of HyDra, Kismet, Skyz and Sib closed the regular season fifth in the table but produced their best LAN performance of the year through the upper bracket, dropping a single map across the first three rounds. The grand final ran the full BO9 distance and stretched into the fifth game of an Atlanta lead before NYSL won three straight to lift the trophy.

HyDra was named event MVP, posting a 1.31 K/D across the playoff run and the highest individual Hardpoint engagement rate of any AR at the event. The win was the Subliners' second Major trophy after their CDL Championship 2023 win and a clear marker that they were the team to beat heading into the Allen, TX Champs.
