Organization Overview
French esports flagship · zen and ExoTiiK at the heart of the trio

Team Vitality is a French esports organisation headquartered in Paris. The Rocket League division was launched on February 12, 2018, originally under the Renault Vitality co-brand, and rebranded back to Team Vitality in 2021. The trio of Kaydop, Fairy Peak! and Scrub Killa won the RLCS Season 7 World Championship in 2019, the organisation's first Rocket League title.

The current era is built around striker zen (Alexis Bernier) and defender ExoTiiK (Brice Bigeard), the players who anchored the RLCS 2022-23 World Championship winning roster. They were joined by stizzy (Gaspar Rosalen Andres) for the post-2024 cycle, with Eversax (Benjamin Wagner) coaching.

Vitality have accumulated approximately $2.51M in tracked Rocket League earnings, the highest lifetime total of any organisation actively competing in the game, and the team has held a top-five international power ranking position in nearly every season since 2018.
