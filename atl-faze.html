Organization Overview
Atlanta FaZe → FaZe Vegas · Simp's home since 2019

Atlanta FaZe was unveiled on May 2, 2019 as a founding franchise of the Call of Duty League, owned by FaZe Esports as part of the franchise league's debut twelve-team field. The original 2019 roster of Simp, aBeZy, Cellium and Arcitys, plus head coach Crowder, became the dominant unit of the early franchise era.

The team won the CDL Championship 2021 on Cold War, defeating Toronto Ultra in the grand final, and went on to capture the Esports World Cup 2024 Call of Duty bracket in Riyadh on Modern Warfare III. Atlanta FaZe also made three additional CDL grand finals across the 2021 to 2025 stretch, finishing as the franchise era's most decorated runner-up.

FaZe Esports relocated the franchise to Las Vegas as FaZe Vegas on September 19, 2025. The current 2026 roster is built around Simp and Drazah alongside offseason additions 04 and Abuzah, with Crowder remaining as head coach.
