Organization Overview
Carlos Rodriguez's flagship · NiKo and m0NESY era

G2 Esports is a German esports organization headquartered in Berlin, founded November 2013 by Carlos "ocelote" Rodriguez (Spain) and Jens Hilgers (Germany), the latter also a co-founder of ESL. It is one of the largest multi-game esports organizations in Europe, with sustained presence across League of Legends, VALORANT, Counter-Strike, sim racing, and Rocket League. Alban Dechelotte (France) succeeded Rodriguez as CEO in 2023.

G2's CS roster reached its peak between 2020 and 2024 with the NiKo, m0NESY, huNter- trio. They reached the PGL Major Stockholm 2021 grand final, lost 0-2 to Natus Vincere, then won IEM Cologne 2023, IEM Katowice 2023, and the BLAST Premier World Final 2022 and 2024.

m0NESY transferred to Team Falcons on April 14, 2025 and NiKo joined Falcons in January 2025, opening a transitional rebuild around longtime IGL huNter- and a younger Israeli/Spanish/Slovak core.
