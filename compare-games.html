Notes on the numbers

Career earnings sourced from publicly reported tournament prize pools. Streamer income, salaries, and sponsorships are not included · they would dwarf prize money for most LoL and Valorant players. "Peak achievement" is editorial. Multi-year dominance (Faker, Bugha) and one-night legendary runs (Demon1) sit on the same axis. That tension is the point.
