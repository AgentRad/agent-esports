MrSavage

From AgentTV, the serious source for esports.

Martin Foss Andersen (born November 12, 2004), known online as MrSavage, is a Norwegian professional Fortnite player currently competing for 100 Thieves. With approximately $1.4 million in career earnings across 158 events, he is one of the longest-active and most consistent EU pros of the post-World Cup era.[1]

Andersen famously qualified for the 2019 Fortnite World Cup Solo Final at age 14, finishing 6th overall and winning $750,000. He partnered with Benjyfishy in the Duo Final, finishing 4th for an additional $1.125 million.

Career

Andersen was identified as a young prodigy in 2018 and signed with Atlantis prior to the 2019 World Cup. He moved to NRG following the World Cup and has been with 100 Thieves since 2022. He continues to compete actively in EU FNCS Majors as of 2026.
