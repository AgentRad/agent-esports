★ 2025 FNCS Global Champion · French EU veteran · Team Liquid

Andilex

From AgentTV, the serious source for esports. Last updated May 7, 2026 by AGENT staff.

Alexandre Christophe (born January 31, 2003), known online as Andilex, is a French professional Fortnite Battle Royale player currently competing for Team Liquid. He won the 2025 FNCS Global Championship paired with Canadian IGL Mero. With approximately $582,951 in lifetime tournament earnings, he is one of the longest-tenured EU competitors at the top level.[1]

Andilex first broke through during the FNCS: Chapter 2 Season 2 era, winning the EU Grand Finals in 2020. After several years competing in EU duos and trios, the 2025 cross-region pairing with Mero · facilitated by Liquid's NA-EU player swap program · made him the first French player to win an FNCS Global Championship.

Competitive career

Andilex won the FNCS: Chapter 2 Season 2 EU Grand Finals in 2020 ($35,000), placed 2nd at C2S4 Grand Finals ($31,000), and finished 4th at C2S5 ($40,000). He competed across multiple EU rosters before joining MGA Esport as Head of Fortnite in late 2025, then transferred to Team Liquid for the 2025 GC run.[1]

2025 Global Championship

Paired with Mero, Andilex won the 2025 FNCS Global Championship · the first ever GC won by a cross-region NA / EU duo. The pair's victory cemented Liquid's return to the top of the Fortnite ecosystem and represented vindication for Andilex after multiple near-misses across his career.
