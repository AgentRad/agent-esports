Organization Overview
Short, dominant Apex run

DarkZero Esports entered Apex Legends on July 5, 2022 by signing the trio of Zer0, Genburten and Sikezz. Within five days the lineup won the 2022 ALGS Championship in Raleigh ($500,000), one of the fastest org-to-Champion turnarounds in any major esports title.

DarkZero added the 2023 ALGS Year 3 Split 2 Playoffs in London ($300,000), the 2024 Year 4 Split 1 Pro League NA ($20,000), and finished 2nd at the 2024 Year 4 Split 1 Playoffs ($160,000).

The Apex division was disbanded on May 28, 2024 when Zer0 and Genburten signed with Team Falcons. Across less than two years of operation, DarkZero accumulated $1,044,719 in tracked earnings.
