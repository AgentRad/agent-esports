Organization Overview
Brazilian content and esports powerhouse

LOUD is a Brazilian esports and content organization founded on February 9, 2019, headquartered in São Paulo. The org joined Valorant on January 31, 2022 and immediately built one of the most decorated rosters in the game's history.

LOUD's iconic 2022 roster of aspas, saadhak, Less, Sacy and pANcada won VALORANT Champions 2022 in Istanbul, defeating OpTic Gaming 3-1 in the Grand Final on September 18, 2022. aspas was named tournament MVP, and the team became the first Latin American organization to win a global VCT title.

The 2026 roster is a fresh Brazilian build led by IGL cauanzin, with lukxo, Virtyy, Darker and erde. Coach Romanilly was hired in 2024 and remains in charge through the rebuild.
