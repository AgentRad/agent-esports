Team Liquid is a Dutch-American esports organization founded in 2000 as a StarCraft community website. Headquartered in Utrecht, Netherlands and Santa Monica, California, it is one of the most established esports organizations globally with active divisions in League of Legends, CS2, Dota 2, Apex Legends, VALORANT, Fortnite and more.

Team Liquid's Fortnite division won the 2025 FNCS Global Championship in Antwerp via the duo of Mero and Andilex, taking home $400,000 combined. Other notable past Liquid Fortnite competitors include 72hrs (2018-2020) and Reverse2k.

7 active players across NA East and EU regions. Mero and Andilex anchor the duo lineup.
