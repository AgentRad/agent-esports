Organization Overview
Brazilian-rooted org with longtime ALGS presence

FURIA Esports was founded on August 10, 2017 in Brazil and entered Apex Legends on October 15, 2021. The org's defining moment came on July 10, 2022, when its lineup placed 2nd at the ALGS Championship in Raleigh for $300,000.

FURIA cycled through multiple lineups over the next three years. Albralelie played for the org from July to December 2023. The final lineup of ImMadness, Keon and Knoqd, with coach Jxmo, finished 8th at the 2025 ALGS Championship for $60,000.

The Apex division was disbanded on August 2, 2025. As of May 2026 the org has not announced a return to competitive Apex.
