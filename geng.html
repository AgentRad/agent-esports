Organization Overview
Heir to the Samsung Galaxy lineage

Gen.G Esports is a South Korean esports organization headquartered in Seoul, with offices in Los Angeles and Shanghai. The organization was formed in 2017 as KSV Esports and acquired the Samsung Galaxy League of Legends roster, taking on the lineage of the 2017 World Champions. The organization rebranded to Gen.G in early 2018.

The team's modern identity is built around mid laner Chovy, who joined for the 2022 season, and ADC Ruler, who returned to the org for 2024 after a year with JD Gaming. Gen.G won the 2024 Mid-Season Invitational in Chengdu against Bilibili Gaming and successfully defended the title at MSI 2025 in Vancouver against T1.

Gen.G fields rosters across League of Legends, Valorant, Fortnite, PUBG, MLB The Show and Honor of Kings, and has been one of the most international Korean orgs by footprint and brand partnerships.
