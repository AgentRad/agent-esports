Quest Esports is a United Arab Emirates-based esports organization founded in 2021, operating across multiple competitive titles including Fortnite, Dota 2, PUBG Mobile, and Apex Legends. The org maintains a competitive EU Fortnite presence backed by MENA capital, and is best known in the Fortnite scene as the previous home of Swedish star Pixie prior to his December 2024 transfer to HavoK by Vitality.

Quest's strategy has emphasized scouting top young EU talent and providing player development before higher-tier organizations sign them away. The org has rotated through multiple Fortnite competitors across 2022-2025 in this development model.

Organizational Profile

Quest Esports operates across multiple esports titles and is one of the visible MENA-region orgs alongside Twisted Minds, Team Falcons, and Geek Slate. Its talent-development model contrasts with the recruit-and-retain model favored by older orgs like Dignitas.
