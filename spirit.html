Organization Overview
CIS dynasty · donk era

Team Spirit is a multi-game esports organization founded in 2015 by Nikita Chukalin. Originally headquartered in Moscow, Russia, the org relocated its main operations to Belgrade, Serbia in 2022 following geopolitical complications around CIS tier-one esports.

Spirit's CS2 roster is built around teenage phenom donk, the 2024 HLTV Player of the Year. Together with sh1ro, zont1x, IGL magixx and tN1R, the team won the Perfect World Shanghai Major 2024 · the second CS2 Major and the org's first.

Spirit are also the only org to have won The International (Dota 2, 2021) and a Counter-Strike Major, alongside extensive trophy hauls in Apex and PUBG.
