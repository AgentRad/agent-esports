Organization Overview
French esports flagship · ZywOo's home since 2018

Team Vitality is a French esports organization headquartered in Paris. Founded in 2013 by Fabien "Neo" Devide and Nicolas Maurer, Vitality entered Counter-Strike on October 8, 2018 by signing the all-French roster that introduced 18-year-old ZywOo to the global stage.

Vitality won their first Major at BLAST.tv Paris Major 2023 on home soil with a roster anchored by ZywOo, apEX, dupreeh, Magisk and Spinx. After a 2024 transitional rebuild they assembled the ropz, ZywOo, apEX, flameZ, mezii roster that won the back-to-back BLAST.tv Austin Major 2025 and StarLadder Budapest Major 2025.

The team has held the HLTV world #1 ranking continuously since August 2024 and is widely considered the strongest CS2-era roster assembled to date.
