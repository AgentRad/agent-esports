Organization Overview
"Born to Win" · Ukrainian institution since 2009

Natus Vincere (Latin for "Born to Win"), commonly abbreviated NaVi, is a Ukrainian esports organization founded December 17, 2009 by Oleksandr "ZeroGravity" Kokhanovskyi. It is the longest-running tier-one Counter-Strike organization with continuous representation since 2010 and the first multi-game org to win all three of the WCG, ESWC and Intel Extreme Masters CS world championships in a single calendar year (2010).

In its modern era, NaVi was home to s1mple from August 2016 to mid-2023 and won the PGL Major Stockholm 2021 · the only Major win in CS:GO history without a single map dropped. They followed this up with the PGL Major Copenhagen 2024, becoming the first ever Counter-Strike 2 Major champions with a fully rebuilt roster anchored by Aleksib, b1t, and AWPer w0nderful.

NaVi has accumulated over $12.49 million in tracked CS earnings, the most of any active organization, and is the lead institution in the Ukrainian esports scene.
