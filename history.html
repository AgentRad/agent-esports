WORLD
CUP '19
🏆 Fortnite World Cup · Arthur Ashe Stadium NYC
Fortnite World Cup Finals 2019
July 26-28, 2019 · The single-largest esports prize pool in history at the time. Open online qualifiers led to the LAN finals at Arthur Ashe Stadium, NYC. Sold out three days, 23,000+ in attendance.
Solo Champion: Bugha (Kyle Giersdorf, 16) · 59 points, $3,000,000
2nd: Psalm (33 pts, $1.8M) · 3rd: EpikWhale (32 pts, $1.2M)
Duo Champions: Aqua & Nyhrox · 51 points, $3,000,000 ($1.5M each)
2nd: Rojo & Wolfiez ($2.25M)
Creative: Airwaks & RL Grime · Pro-Am music collab winner
All solo finalists got minimum $50K · all duo finals teams got minimum $100K.
$30,000,000
Total Prize Pool

Skirmish Series · Genesis Event
Summer Skirmish Series
8-week competitive series · Online + LAN events including a $1.5M event at PAX West. The first $8M prize pool in Fortnite competitive history. The series that proved competitive Fortnite was viable at scale.
Featured the first generation of Fortnite pros · Tfue, Cloak, Nick Eh 30, Ninja, Ghost Aydan, Liquid 72hrs, NRG MrSavage, Faze Cloak. Tfue emerged as one of the early dominant players.
