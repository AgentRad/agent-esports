FaZe Clan is an American esports and entertainment organization headquartered in Los Angeles. Founded May 30, 2010 as a Call of Duty trickshot collective, the organization grew into one of the most recognizable brands in gaming, becoming a publicly-traded company (NASDAQ: FAZE) in 2022.

FaZe's Fortnite division has been one of the most prominent in the game's history, having signed many of the era-defining players including Tfue (2018 to 2020), Mongraal (2019 to 2024), Peterbot (2022 to 2024), Pollo (2023 to 2024), and Cented. While the duo of Peterbot and Pollo won the 2024 FNCS Global Championship while on FaZe, both have since departed for Team Falcons.
