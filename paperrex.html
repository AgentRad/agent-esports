Organization Overview
Pacific powerhouse

Paper Rex is a Singapore- and Indonesia-based esports organization founded on January 1, 2020. The Valorant division was created on July 20, 2020 and the org has competed exclusively in Valorant ever since, making them one of the only major Valorant-only franchises in the world.

Built around the long-tenured duo of f0rsakeN (Indonesia) and Jinggg (Singapore), Paper Rex has been a top-3 international team in every VCT cycle since 2022. The team won VCT Masters Toronto 2025 on May 25, 2025, defeating FNATIC 3-1 in the Grand Final, the org's first international VCT title.

Paper Rex finished as runner-up at VALORANT Champions 2023 in Los Angeles, losing 1-3 to Evil Geniuses, and reached the semifinals at VALORANT Champions 2025. The 2026 roster retains the Jinggg-f0rsakeN core with d4v41 as IGL, plus newer signings something and invy.
