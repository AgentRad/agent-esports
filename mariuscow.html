MariusCOW

From AgentTV, the serious source for esports.

Marius Wendt (born November 14, 2006), known online as MariusCOW, is a Danish professional Fortnite player currently competing for Gentle Mates in Europe. With approximately $372,000 in career tournament earnings, he has established himself as one of the top EU prospects of the 2024-2026 era.[1]

MariusCOW won the FNCS 2026 Major 2 EU title and finished 6th at FNCS Major 3 2025. He has consistently placed top-10 across multiple FNCS regional finals.

Career

Wendt qualified for his first FNCS Grand Finals in Chapter 4 Season 2 at age 16. He signed with Gentle Mates in 2025 and has remained with the French organization through the 2026 season.

His trio with Pixie (Iceland) and Vanyak (France) has been one of the more recognizable EU lineups across the duos era, regularly making deep runs in Cash Cup and FNCS Major brackets.
