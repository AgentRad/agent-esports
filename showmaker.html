ShowMaker

From AgentTV, the serious source for esports. Last updated May 7, 2026 by AGENT staff.

Heo Su (Korean: 허수; born September 30, 2000), better known as ShowMaker, is a South Korean professional League of Legends player who plays mid lane for Dplus KIA (formerly Damwon Gaming) in the LCK. He is a 2020 League of Legends World Champion and a two-time LCK MVP.[1][2]

ShowMaker has played his entire professional career for the same organization, signing with Damwon Gaming in 2018 and remaining as the team rebranded to DWG KIA in 2021 and to Dplus KIA in 2023. The team won Worlds 2020 in Shanghai by defeating Suning 3-1, ending a three-year drought of LCK Worlds wins.[2]

Early life

Heo Su was born on September 30, 2000 in South Korea. He competed in Korean amateur tournaments before joining Damwon Gaming in early 2018. The team was promoted to the LCK at the end of 2018 after winning the Korean Challengers Korea (CK) league.[1]

Competitive career

Damwon Gaming and 2020 Worlds

Damwon Gaming established itself as a top-tier LCK contender in 2020, with ShowMaker, Canyon, Nuguri, Ghost and BeryL forming one of the most dominant rosters in modern LCK history. Damwon won the 2020 LCK Summer split and the 2020 World Championship in Shanghai, defeating Suning 3-1 in the final. Canyon was named Worlds 2020 Finals MVP.[2]

DWG KIA era (2021–2022)

Renamed DWG KIA in 2021, the team won the 2021 LCK Spring and Summer splits and reached the Worlds 2021 final in Reykjavik, where they lost to EDward Gaming 2-3. ShowMaker was named LCK Spring 2021 MVP.[2]

Dplus KIA era (2023–present)

Dplus KIA missed Worlds 2023 in a difficult LCK season but returned in 2024 with a deep playoff run that ended in the LCK Spring playoffs. The team continues to build around ShowMaker and Canyon as foundational pieces, qualifying for Worlds 2025 where they exited in the group stage.[3]

Legacy and impact

ShowMaker is widely considered one of the most consistent mid laners of his generation and is the only player to have won both LCK Spring MVP and Summer MVP under the modern format. He is a foundational player of the post-Faker generation and a key figure in the recovery of LCK at Worlds in 2020.[2]

Personal life

ShowMaker is based in Seoul. He maintains a public stream presence on AfreecaTV and is publicly close with longtime teammate Canyon. He is known for an extremely disciplined practice schedule.[1]
