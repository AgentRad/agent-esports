Aqua

From AgentTV, the serious source for esports.

David Wang (born July 14, 2002), known online as Aqua, is an Austrian professional Fortnite player. Alongside Norwegian partner Nyhrox, he won the 2019 Fortnite World Cup Duo Finals, sharing a $3 million grand prize ($1.5M each). With approximately $2.2 million in lifetime tournament earnings, he is among the highest-earning Fortnite players in history.[1]

Wang competed throughout the 2018 to 2024 era and was known for his calculating, methodical playstyle that contrasted with the high-aggression European school of the Mongraal era.

2019 World Cup Duo Final

Wang and Nyhrox entered the Duo Finals on July 27, 2019 as a relatively under-recognized European pair. They closed the day with 51 points across six games, edging out the British duo of Rojo and Wolfiez (39 pts) to take home the $3 million top prize. Their victory remains one of the most celebrated underdog wins in esports history.

Post-World Cup career

Wang competed across the FNCS Chapter 2 trios era and the Chapter 3 duos era, regularly placing top-10 in EU Grand Finals. He retired from competitive play in 2024 with $2.2M in tracked earnings.
