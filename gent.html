Gent

From AgentTV, the serious source for esports. Last updated May 7, 2026 by AGENT staff.

Rigoberto Padilla (born January 11, 1999), known online as Gent, is an American professional Apex Legends player and in-game leader currently signed to GodLike Esports. He is best known for his tenures as IGL for G2 Esports and TSM, and for finishing 7th at the 2026 ALGS Championship in Sapporo for $80,000.[1]

Padilla has competed continuously since 2020, with stints at AimAssist, SolaFide, G2, Tripods, Pioneers and TSM. As of May 2026 his tracked tournament earnings total $112,325.[2]

Early career

Padilla was born January 11, 1999. He entered competitive Apex with AimAssist in January 2020 and developed his IGL identity across the early NA grassroots circuit.

Competitive career

G2 Esports (2021-2022)

Padilla joined G2 Esports on June 1, 2021 and IGL'd the org's NA Apex roster through October 2022. He placed 4th at the 2022 Y2 Split 2 Pro League NA ($10,500).

Tripods era

He played for Tripods in two tenures (2022-2024 and late 2024) and briefly for Pioneers in 2024, building a reputation as a steady IGL who could squeeze high placements out of underdog rosters.

TSM (2025-2026)

Padilla signed with TSM on April 28, 2025 alongside Dooplex and Panic. He led TSM to a 7th-place finish at the 2026 ALGS Championship in Sapporo ($80,000), and 14th at the 2025 Riyadh Midseason Playoffs ($22,000), before TSM disbanded its Apex division on February 1, 2026. He briefly joined Team Burger before signing with GodLike Esports on April 29, 2026.[3]

Reputation

Within NA Apex, Gent is regarded as one of the more analytical IGLs in the region. His 2026 Championship run with TSM was the swan song of one of the most storied org names in Apex.
