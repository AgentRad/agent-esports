Wave Esports is an Austrian / DACH-region esports organization founded July 7, 2019. Headquartered in Austria, the organization has accumulated approximately $1,854,369 in tracked Fortnite earnings · among the highest of any DACH-region team. Despite operating with a smaller two-player active roster, the org has maintained a continuous presence in the EU FNCS qualifier system since 2020.

The organization is led by CEO Luke and coached by RazZzero0o. Notable former players include Flickzy, JannisZ, and Noahreyli, who collectively built the org's competitive reputation across 2020-2024.
