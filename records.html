FN Fortnite Records Fortnite hub

The early years of competitive Fortnite still anchor most of the title's biggest single numbers. The 2019 World Cup was a one-off event that has not been re-staged at anything close to the same scale, and its prize pool, age records and individual payouts continue to top the all-time list seven years later. CS Counter-Strike 2 Records CS2 hub

Counter-Strike's record book belongs to two players. Mathieu Herbaut and Oleksandr Kostyliev have between them held the HLTV #1 spot for seven of the last eight years, and their head-to-head on individual ratings is one of the longest-running arguments in the competitive scene. The only thing breaking up that conversation in 2024 was a 17-year-old who had not played at any tier-one event before the calendar year began.

V Valorant Records Valorant hub

Valorant's record book is younger than the rest, and the marks below mostly belong to the post-franchise era. Two duelists drive most of the conversation: a Brazilian who has the only triple Duelist of the Year on record, and an American who took an underdog roster all the way at Champions 2023. The biggest single payouts in the title's history still come out of the VCT Champions stage, and Los Angeles 2023 set the bar.

LoL League of Legends Records League hub

Most League of Legends records belong to one player, and that player has not retired yet. Lee Sang-hyeok's professional career began on April 7, 2013, and the entries below describe a body of work no other player in any team-based esport has matched. The 2023 to 2025 T1 three-peat at Worlds is, on top of that, the first such run since SKT's near-three-peat almost a decade earlier.

AL Apex Legends Records Apex hub

Apex's competitive history is shorter than the rest of the games on this page, and the record book reflects that. Most of the longest-running marks belong to a small group of North American rosters and a handful of pacific-region individual outliers. The biggest single trophy belongs to TSM's 2023 ALGS Championship run; the largest stage to date was the Daiwa House PREMIST DOME in Hokkaido for the Year 4 Championship.

How this list is maintained

Every entry on this page is sourced from a record AgentTV has previously documented on a player wiki, team page, tournament archive or the Fortnite history timeline. We do not include records we cannot ground in an existing AgentTV page. As new marks are set during live events, the records desk updates this page within 24 hours and links back to the originating coverage.
