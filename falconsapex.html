Division Overview
Saudi-backed superteam, top of NA Apex since 2024

Team Falcons launched its Apex Legends division on May 29, 2024 by signing ImperialHal from TSM, plus Genburten and Zer0 from DarkZero. The signing was the highest-profile triple acquisition in Apex history.

The lineup won the 2024 Y4 Split 2 Pro League NA ($20,000) and the 2025 Y5 Split 1 Pro League Americas ($20,000), then took the 2025 ALGS Open ($300,000). Genburten left in September 2024, and Zer0 became inactive in September 2025. The current 2026 lineup features ImperialHal, Wxltzy (Ben Walton, Australia) and Gild (Zachary Dennis, USA), with Draugr coaching.

Falcons placed 3rd at the 2025 ALGS Championship ($210,000) and 2nd at the 2026 Championship in Sapporo ($300,000). As of May 2026 the org sits in the top three of NA power rankings.
