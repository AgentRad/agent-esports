Organization Overview
European multi-game pioneer

FNATIC is a British esports organization founded on July 23, 2004, headquartered in London. The org joined Valorant on February 3, 2021 and is one of just two organizations to win two VCT Masters titles, alongside Sentinels.

FNATIC famously lost the Grand Final of the inaugural VCT Masters Reykjavik 2021 0-3 to Sentinels before rebuilding around Boaster, the league's longest-tenured IGL. The team won Masters Tokyo 2023 by defeating Evil Geniuses 3-2, then added Masters EMEA Stage 2 2024 with a roster led by Boaster, Alfajer, Derke, Chronicle and Leo.

FNATIC reached the Grand Final of VALORANT Champions 2025, losing 1-3 to Paper Rex. The 2026 roster retains Boaster and Alfajer as the experienced core, with Polish duelist kaajak, NA-imported initiator crashies and French controller CyvOph.
