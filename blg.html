Organization Overview
Built around superstar mid laner Knight

Bilibili Gaming (commonly abbreviated as BLG) is a Chinese esports organization headquartered in Shanghai. Founded in 2017 by video platform Bilibili Inc., the team purchased an LPL franchise slot in 2018 and spent its first several seasons as a mid-tier LPL squad.

The org's current era began in 2023 with the signing of mid laner Knight from TOP Esports on a record-breaking contract. BLG reached the MSI 2023 final (lost to JD Gaming), the MSI 2024 final (lost to Gen.G) and the 2024 World Championship final in London, where they lost to T1 2-3 in a five-game series.

BLG won the 2024 LPL Summer split and qualified for Worlds 2025. The team is led by veteran top laner Bin and former JDG support ON.
