Organization Overview
JD.com-backed Beijing LPL powerhouse

JD Gaming (commonly abbreviated as JDG) is a Chinese esports organization headquartered in Beijing. Founded in 2017 by Chinese e-commerce giant JD.com, the team purchased an LPL franchise slot in the league's transition to a permanent partnership model and has remained one of the LPL's top-tier teams since.

JDG's most successful season was 2023, when the team signed Korean ADC Ruler from Gen.G and won the LPL Spring split, the 2023 Mid-Season Invitational in London (defeating Bilibili Gaming 3-1) and the 2023 LPL Summer split. JDG came one game short of the so-called "Golden Road" before being eliminated by T1 in the Worlds 2023 semifinals.

The 2024 to 2025 seasons saw a partial roster rebuild after Ruler returned to Korea. JDG remains an LPL playoff regular and is led by veteran top laner 369 and longtime jungler Kanavi.
