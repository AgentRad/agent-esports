Division Overview
Most decorated NA Apex roster history

TSM entered Apex Legends on March 6, 2019, less than two months after the game's launch. The Los Angeles-based organization signed ImperialHal as the cornerstone of its first roster and went on to build the most decorated lineup in North American Apex.

The original lineup of ImperialHal, Albralelie and Reps won the 2019 Preseason Invitational ($105,000) and dominated NA pre-ALGS Apex. Albralelie left full-time in 2020 and was replaced by Verhulst. The ImperialHal-Verhulst-Reps trio became the defining lineup of the early ALGS era.

TSM won the 2023 ALGS Championship in Birmingham on a Match Point run, ending a multi-year wait at the most prestigious LAN. ImperialHal departed for Team Falcons in May 2024, and TSM rebuilt around Gent, Dooplex and Panic before disbanding the Apex division on February 1, 2026.
