Setty

From AgentTV, the serious source for esports. Last updated May 7, 2026 by AGENT staff.

Iwo Zając (born September 8, 2003), known online as Setty, is a Polish professional Fortnite Battle Royale player. Best known as one half of the dominant EU duo with Kami, he won the FNCS Invitational 2022 ($100,000 each), the C2S8 FNCS Grand Finals: Europe ($100,000), and placed 2nd at the FNCS 2023 Global Championship ($325,000 each). With approximately $1,379,795 in lifetime tournament earnings, he ranks among the highest-earning EU competitors of all time.[1]

Setty last competed for Manchester City Esports until January 2025 and has since been listed as teamless. He continues to stream and compete in Cash Cup events.

Competitive career

Setty's defining era came partnered with Polish duo-mate Kami across 2021-2023. The pair won the C2S8 FNCS Grand Finals (EU) in 2021 alongside Teeq, then won the FNCS Invitational 2022 in Raleigh ($100,000 each), and finished 2nd at the FNCS 2023 Global Championship in Copenhagen ($325,000 each).[1]

Setty's style · methodical building paired with disciplined endgame positioning · made the duo a fixture in the FNCS top-3 across multiple seasons, and his earnings rank in the top tier of European Fortnite history alongside TaySon and MrSavage.
