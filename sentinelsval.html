Organization Overview
North American esports brand

Sentinels is an American esports organization founded in 2016 and headquartered in Los Angeles. The org joined Valorant on March 9, 2020 and rose to global prominence in 2021 by winning VCT Masters Reykjavik, the inaugural international Valorant tournament, with the iconic TenZ, ShahZaM, SicK, dapr and zombs roster.

Sentinels is one of just two organizations in Valorant history to win two Masters titles, having added Masters Madrid in March 2024 with TenZ, Zekken, Sacy, johnqt and Zellsis. TenZ retired from active play in September 2024 and remains with the org as a content creator.

The 2026 roster features johnqt as IGL, with reduxx, JonahP, Jerrwin and Brazilian newcomer cortezia. Head coach Ewok was hired from VCT EMEA before the 2025 season.
