Ajerss

From AgentTV, the serious source for esports.

Aidan Joseph Bernero (born November 19, 2005), known online as Ajerss, is an American professional Fortnite player currently competing for Gen.G Esports (signed January 8, 2026). With approximately $654,000 in lifetime tournament earnings, he is among the top NA Central competitors.[1]

Bernero is a two-time FNCS Major regional champion: 2025 Major 1 NA Central (with Acorn and Pollo) and 2023 Major 3 NA (with Khanada). He has competed for FUSION, G2 Esports, Counter Logic Gaming, Fnatic, and Elite Esports before joining Gen.G.
