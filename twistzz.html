Twistzz

From AgentTV, the serious source for esports. Last updated May 7, 2026 by AGENT staff.

Russel David Kevin Van Dulken (born November 14, 1999), known online as Twistzz, is a Canadian professional Counter-Strike 2 player currently signed to FaZe Clan. A consistent rifler known for his entry presence and clutch capacity, he is the only player to have won the Intel Grand Slam with two different organizations: Team Liquid in 2019 and FaZe Clan in 2023.[1][2]

Van Dulken's career has alternated between Team Liquid and FaZe Clan across three separate signings, and he has appeared in two Major grand finals, winning the PGL Major Antwerp 2022 against Natus Vincere. He has accumulated approximately $1.84 million in tracked earnings, placing him among the top-earning North American CS players in history.[1][3]

Early life

Van Dulken was born November 14, 1999 in Pinawa, Manitoba, Canada. He began his competitive career through Canadian and North American mixed teams in 2015, signing his first amateur contract at age 16.[1]

Career

Early career (2015 to 2017)

Van Dulken passed through Tectonic, SapphireKelownaDotCom, Team AGG, KKona, Team SoloMid and Misfits Gaming before being signed to Team Liquid on April 14, 2017.[1]

Team Liquid (2017 to 2020)

Van Dulken's first stint with Team Liquid produced the most decorated North American Counter-Strike season in history. The roster of Stewie2K, NAF, EliGE, nitr0 and Twistzz won Intel Grand Slam Season 2 in 2019 inside a 63-day stretch, lifting four S-Tier trophies in succession. He was awarded HLTV MVP medals at ESL One New York 2018 and IEM Sydney 2019, and finished HLTV #9 in 2019.[2]

FaZe Clan (2021 to 2023)

On January 30, 2021, Van Dulken joined FaZe Clan, completing the karrigan, ropz, rain, broky roster widely considered the most complete European international lineup of the post-Astralis era. Together they won the PGL Major Antwerp 2022 over Natus Vincere, completed Intel Grand Slam Season 4 in 2023, and lifted IEM Katowice 2022 and BLAST Premier Spring Final 2022 in the same calendar year.[3]

Return to Liquid (2023 to 2025)

Van Dulken returned to Team Liquid on December 7, 2023 to anchor a partly Western-led rebuild around NAF and YEKINDAR. Liquid produced multiple S-Tier semifinal runs and qualified for both the PGL Major Copenhagen 2024 and BLAST.tv Austin Major 2025 playoffs.[1]

FaZe Clan return (2025 to present)

On September 20, 2025, Van Dulken rejoined FaZe Clan, reuniting with karrigan, broky and the FaZe core in place of frozen. The roster reached the grand final of the StarLadder Budapest Major 2025 later that fall, falling 1-3 to Team Vitality.[4]

Legacy and awards

Van Dulken's two Intel Grand Slams across two organizations is a record unique to him in Counter-Strike. His PGL Antwerp 2022 victory remains FaZe Clan's only Major win and one of only three Majors won by a fully international roster. He has been ranked HLTV top-20 in six different years, a level of longevity matched by very few non-superstar riflers.[2][3]

Personal life

Van Dulken splits time between Manitoba and the FaZe Clan training facility in Berlin. He has been a vocal commentator on roster moves and player welfare, particularly during transition periods between organizations.[1]
