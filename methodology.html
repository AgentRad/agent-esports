Methodology and Data Sources

How AgentTV sources, verifies, and presents the data behind every player profile, team profile, tournament archive, and live coverage page.

Editorial principles

AgentTV is built to be a serious, citation-first esports source. Every claim of fact on a player or team profile is sourced from an established competitive esports reference. We do not invent stats, results, or biographical details. When a fact cannot be verified, we either omit it or label it as estimated.

Primary data sources

The structured data that powers our player profiles, team profiles, tournament results, and rankings is sourced from the following references. Each player profile that has equivalent third-party tracker pages includes one-click links to those trackers in the right rail of the wiki.

Liquipedia

The bulk of biographical data, current rosters, transfer histories, tournament results, prize distributions, and event metadata comes from Liquipedia. Liquipedia content is licensed under CC-BY-SA 3.0, which permits reuse with attribution. Every player photo we display is hotlinked from Liquipedia commons with a "Photo via Liquipedia (CC-BY-SA 3.0)" caption.

Esports Earnings

Lifetime career earnings totals are cross-referenced with esportsearnings.com. Where the two sources differ, we typically defer to Liquipedia for player metadata and use Esports Earnings as a sanity check on dollar figures.

Fortnite live stats trackers

For Fortnite player profiles specifically, we provide one-click links from each player wiki to the major live stats trackers in the community:

  • Fortnite Tracker (tracker.gg) for general profile, K/D, win rate, mode breakdown
  • Osirion.gg for advanced scrim stats and competitive performance
  • Blitz.gg for analytics overlay and benchmark stats
  • Fortnite.gg for community leaderboards and item shop history

AgentTV does not host or republish the live stat data from these services. We only link to the relevant profile on the source site. Live stat data on those sites belongs to the respective operator.

Tournament results and brackets

Tournament archives draw from a combination of Liquipedia and the official publisher results pages (Epic Games for FNCS, HLTV for CS2, the Riot esports portals for Valorant and LoL, EA Apex for ALGS). Each tournament archive page lists its references at the bottom of the body.

Photography

Player and tournament photos come from two licensed sources:

  • Liquipedia commons · CC-BY-SA 3.0, attributed in caption
  • Unsplash · for atmospheric and venue photography, free for commercial use under the Unsplash License

Team and tournament logos shown on team profile pages and homepage tiles are stylized brand-color shields rather than reproductions of the official trademarked logos. At production scale, AgentTV would license official logos through publisher and organization media kits.

Data freshness

Static profile data (real names, birth dates, career earnings totals, transfer history) is updated as new information becomes verifiable. Live coverage and live stats are pulled from the linked third-party trackers, not stored or modified by AgentTV. Power Rankings and rolling 90-day earnings on AgentTV-hosted leaderboards are updated weekly.

Editorial corrections

When the AgentTV editorial team identifies a factual error in a profile, the page is updated and a brief change note is added to the page footer if the correction is material (for example, a wrong real name or a misattributed tournament win). For minor corrections (typos, formatting), no note is added.
