How the Eight Got Here
Two teams from each league plus regional kickoff results

VCT Masters Toronto 2026 invited the top two teams from each of the four international leagues based on Stage 1 standings. The Americas sent Sentinels as the top seed alongside LOUD, with NRG and Evil Geniuses qualifying through the Stage 1 group runner-up slots. Pacific shipped Paper Rex at the top of the conference and T1 on the second seed, with DRX taking the play-in slot. EMEA's lone qualifier was FNATIC after a clean Stage 1 group, with the EMEA second seed running through the play-in. China's bracket spots travel separately to a regional Master event in 2026.

Group stage was scrapped this season. All eight teams enter the playoff bracket directly from arrival, four UB Bo3 quarterfinals on Wednesday and Thursday, with the standard double-elimination flow following on through the Sunday Bo5 grand final.
