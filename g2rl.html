Organization Overview
Berlin esports brand · long-tenured RL presence

G2 Esports is a multinational esports organisation headquartered in Berlin, Germany. Founded in November 2013 as Gamers2 by Carlos "ocelote" Rodriguez and rebranded to G2 Esports in 2015, the organisation entered Rocket League on September 7, 2016 by acquiring the iBUYPOWER roster.

The G2 RL division reached its first RLCS grand final at the RLCS 2021-22 World Championship in Dallas, finishing runner-up to Gen.G Mobil1 Racing. They captured their first Major at the RLCS 2021-22 Winter Split Major on March 27, 2022 with a roster featuring JKnaps, Chicago and Rizzo.

The trio of Atomic, BeastMode and Daniel formed the G2 Stride brand for the RLCS 2024 season and lifted the RLCS 2024 Major 2: London on June 23, 2024 over Team Falcons 4-1 at the Copper Box Arena. The team subsequently advanced to the grand final of the RLCS 2024 World Championship in Fort Worth, where they were defeated by Team BDS.

On November 7, 2024 the organisation released its full Rocket League roster as part of a wider esports portfolio review. G2 has continued to operate a competitive Rocket League brand through 2025 and 2026, and they have accumulated approximately $2.17M in tracked Rocket League earnings, the second-highest lifetime total of any organisation in the game.
