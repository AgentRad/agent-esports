Nyhrox

From AgentTV, the serious source for esports.

Emil Bergquist Pedersen (born September 8, 2002), known online as Nyhrox, is a retired Norwegian professional Fortnite player. Alongside Austrian partner Aqua, he won the 2019 Fortnite World Cup Duo Finals at age 16, taking home $1.5 million.[1]

Pedersen accumulated approximately $1.96 million in career tournament earnings before retiring from competitive Fortnite in 2023. His World Cup victory remains one of the most celebrated upset stories in esports history.

Career

Pedersen began competing in late 2018 through European online qualifiers. The Aqua + Nyhrox duo emerged through World Cup qualifiers and was not considered a pre-event favorite. Their dominant Saturday performance, with 51 points to runner-up Rojo & Wolfiez's 39, sealed the championship.

Post-World Cup, Pedersen competed for several European organizations including Atlantis. He continued competing through Chapter 3's duos era before officially retiring in 2023.
