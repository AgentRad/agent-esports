Acorn

From AGENT, the serious source for esports.

Brandon Macias, known online as Acorn, is an American professional Fortnite Battle Royale player currently competing for Team Falcons. Previously a member of FaZe Clan and Dignitas, Macias is currently leading the FNCS 2026 Major 1 Summit with duo partner Pollo at 770 points.[1]

Macias is among the most consistent NA East fraggers of the modern era, with $924,830 in lifetime tournament earnings across 82 events. His verified Twitch channel sits at twitch.tv/acorn, and he uses Epic Creator Code "Acornz".

Career

FaZe Clan (2022·2023)

Macias signed with FaZe Clan in early 2022 alongside teammates Cented and Peterbot. The trio became one of the most consistent NA East rosters of the Chapter 3 and Chapter 4 eras, regularly placing top-10 in regional FNCS finals.

Dignitas (2024·2025)

Macias moved to Dignitas in late 2023, competing under the Dignitas banner at the FNCS 2025 Global Championship in Antwerp. The portrait used on this page was captured during that event, photographed in Dignitas team colors.[1]

Team Falcons (2025·present)

Falcons signed Macias in late 2025 alongside Ajerss, completing the Pollo + Acorn + Ajerss trio that currently leads the NA East FNCS standings. The trio has held the #1 position throughout the 2026 Major 1 cycle.
