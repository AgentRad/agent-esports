100 Thieves is an American esports and lifestyle organization founded November 16, 2017 by professional Call of Duty player Matthew "Nadeshot" Haag. Headquartered in Los Angeles, the org has built an unusually strong cultural presence beyond competitive esports, with apparel and content arms that rival its team operations.

100 Thieves' Fortnite division has fielded competitive rosters since 2018. The current EU lead is MrSavage (Martin Foss Andersen), one of the longest-active EU pros and a 2019 World Cup top-10 finisher in both Solo and Duo events. NA East players Stretch and Avery anchor the North American roster.
